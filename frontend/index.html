CS153 · hybrid perception + LLM

Hybrid Mastering Agent

A fine-tuned PANNs model listens for eight mastering problems, an LLM reasons over those scores plus raw DSP measurements, and a Pedalboard chain renders the fix — looping up to three times. Pick a demo track (optionally degraded to give the agent work) or upload your own, then A/B the result and read what the agent decided.
